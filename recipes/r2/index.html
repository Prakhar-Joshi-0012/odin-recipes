Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken leg and chest
  • Oil
  • Onion chopped (2)
  • tomato chopped (2)
  • ground spices
  • honey and lemon juice
  • Garlic paste

Directions

  1. Marinate the Chicken pieces with oil and ground spices.
    Add salt, pepper and garlic paste to the marination.
    let the marination rest for 2 hours

  2. Fry the onions in the skillet till golden brown.
    Add the marinated chicken pieces(* Only add pieces not marination)
    and let it sit for 5 - 10 minutes.
    Add the tomatoes to the skillet and add 4 cups of water.

  3. Add salt and pepper to the curry
    Wait until the curry thickens.
